School History

Solent School was established in 1990 with a mission to provide excellent education to students in the local community. Over the years, it has grown into a well-respected institution known for its commitment to academic excellence and student development.

School Ambitions

Our ambition is to foster a learning environment that encourages students to achieve their full potential. We aim to inspire and challenge our students to be innovative thinkers, confident communicators, and responsible global citizens.

Solent University Introduction

Solent University is a dynamic and ambitious institution located in the heart of Southampton, UK. Established in 2005, the university has quickly gained a reputation for providing high-quality education and fostering an inclusive and supportive community for its students.

Our Mission and Values

Solent University is committed to providing excellent education that is accessible and relevant to all students. Our mission is to prepare students for successful careers and to contribute positively to society. We value:

  • Academic excellence
  • Innovation and creativity
  • Diversity and inclusivity
  • Community engagement
  • Sustainability

Academic Programs

Solent University offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs across various fields of study, including:

  • Business and Law
  • Art, Design, and Fashion
  • Media and Communications
  • Engineering and Technology
  • Health and Social Sciences

Campus Facilities

Our campus is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities to support student learning and development. Key facilities include:

  • Modern lecture halls and classrooms
  • Extensive library and learning resources
  • Specialized labs and workshops
  • Sports and fitness centers
  • Student accommodation

Student Life

At Solent University, we believe in providing a vibrant and enriching student experience. Our campus hosts a variety of clubs, societies, and events to help students make the most of their time at university. Key aspects of student life include:

  • Over 50 student societies
  • Regular social and cultural events
  • Volunteer and leadership opportunities
  • Support services for health and well-being

Global Opportunities

Solent University is dedicated to providing students with global opportunities. We offer various programs for international study, internships, and exchange programs. Students can gain invaluable experience and develop a global perspective through:

  • Study abroad programs
  • International internships
  • Exchange partnerships with universities worldwide
  • Global research projects
